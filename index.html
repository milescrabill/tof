Reinvision the zoom shot

The days of manual are behind us. Focus on the future with HyperFocus. Move your camera with the world, and we will move the world with your camera.

How HyperFocus works

HyperFocus uses ultrasonic waves to determine the location of the objects your camera is pointing at. With this information, we smoothly move your lens' focus to keep objects clear.

Powered by open source hardware

We use the Arduino Uno R3 microcontroller to compute register the ultrasonic sensor data and operate the stepper motor that adjusts the lens focus.
